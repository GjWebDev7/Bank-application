<h1>Bank Application</h1>
    <p>Welcome to the Bank Application! This JavaScript application is designed to provide users with a simple and intuitive interface to manage their banking operations.</p>
    <h2>Overview</h2>
    <p>This application utilizes JavaScript to create a dynamic banking system. Users can perform various banking operations without the need to navigate through complex interfaces.</p>
    <h2>Features</h2>
    <ol>
        <li><strong>Login System:</strong> Users can securely log in using their username and PIN.</li>
        <li><strong>Transaction History:</strong> View a detailed history of all transactions, including type (deposit or withdrawal), date, and amount.</li>
        <li><strong>Balance Display:</strong> Instantly see the current balance in the account.</li>
        <li><strong>Summary:</strong> Get a comprehensive summary of total income, total outcome, and interest earned.</li>
        <li><strong>Money Transfer:</strong> Seamlessly transfer money to other accounts with just a few clicks.</li>
        <li><strong>Loan Request:</strong> Request a loan if eligible, and receive prompt approval or rejection.</li>
        <li><strong>Account Closure:</strong> Close your account hassle-free, ensuring smooth account management.</li>
    </ol>
    <h2 id="usage">Usage</h2>
    <h3>Logging In</h3>
    <ol>
        <li>Enter your username and PIN in the designated fields.</li>
        <li>Click the "Log In" button to access your account.</li>
    </ol>
